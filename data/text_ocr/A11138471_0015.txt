UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE IMMIGRATION AND NATURALIZATION SERVICE Phoenix, Arizona Date: Jan. 21, 1963 Mrs. Florence Cohen 417 N. 32nd Place, Apt. 5 Phoenix, Arizona Dear Madam: Examination of your petition for naturalization Indicates your child Chery I may possibly have become a United States citizen through your recent naturalize- tion. This Service is authorized to issue a certificate of citizenship to children who have automatically become citizens in that manner. Such a certificate will prove that the child is a citizen, and the records of this Service in Washington will show that fact. If you are interested in obtaining a certificate of citizenship for your child, the enclosed application must be completed In accordance with the instructions in the application, and sent to this office with the required photographs, money order, and documents. After receipt of the application, you will be notified when to appear with the child for Interview. There will be no appearance or hearing in a court, and the certificate will be issued as soon as your child's citizenship has been established. Sincerely, JAMES L. TURNER DISTRICT DIRECTOR Enclosure Form N-334 (4-15-59)
