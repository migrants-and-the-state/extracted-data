For three months, I lived with my son, Dr. Sulaiman Khan who is a resident interne at Southside Hospital in Long Island, N. Y., where I was a patient for those months. As my previously submitted evidence bears out, I suffer from diabetes and chronic kidney failure. To these facts, I swear as the truth. I FURTHER AVER that, it is not only impossible and improbable that I work, but, I do not intend nor, will I, enter into any venture inactively, which will render me remuneration, while a guest of either of my sons, having two here, while in this country.
