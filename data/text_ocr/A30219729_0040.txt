ADDRESS REPORTS You are required by law to notify the Attorney General of your current address each year during the month of January, and to furnish notification of change of address within 10 days from the date of such change. A pen- alty is provided for failure to do so. Forms may be obtained from any post office. Address reports, appli- cations or letters to the Immigration and Naturalization Service, should include the "A" number shown on the other side. BORDER CROSSING CARD This card will be honored for bor- der crossing purposes on condition that the rightful holder is returning to the United States from a temporary visit to Canada or Mexico, not ex- ceeding 6 months, and is not subject to exclusion under any provision of GPO 1952 (-O-228095 the immigration laws.
