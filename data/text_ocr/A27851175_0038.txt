Screened by NARA, 9/22/2023 REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM EXTRACT FROM THE RECORDS DEPOSITED COURT OF PEACE : SAIGON AT THE CLERK'S OFFICE OF SAIGON COURT OF PEACE No. 5730 Date : October 05,1961 DOCUMENT IN LIEU OF BIRTH CERTIFICATE FOR : TRAN MAI In the year of 1961 , the 5th day of October , at 8:00 In front of us, NGUYEN VAN DOANH, President of Saigon Court of Peace, at our office, 115 Nguyen Hue Blvd., and PHAN VAN TU , Court Clerk; as assistant, HAVE APPEARED : 1.- VUONG NGOC 2.- HUONG TUU 3.- DIEP QUOC CHAN These witnesses, after being sworn, affirmed they perfectly know that : TRAN MAI, Female, was born in 1914 at Kwangtung (China) to Mr. ONG LOI (deceased) and Mrs. LAM THI (living). TRANSLATION And the reason for which she could not apply for an ex- tract of birth certificate is that due to the geographical scission of the country. The witnesses have acknowledged all the above, therefore we issued this substitute of birth certificate to the applicant for use within the law, in accordance with the Nov. 17, 1947 Decree of the Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of South Vietnam. The articles 241 and 245 of the Revised Criminal Code punishing false testimony on civil matters from 2 to 5 years imprisonment and fine from 1000 to 3000 piastres have been read to the witnesses and the applicant. We, the Court Clerk and the witnesses have signed below after all the above have been re-read. Signed: illegible CERTIFIED TRUE EXTRACT Saigon, October 06, 1962 The Head-Clerk, I, NGUYEN ANH TUAN, a duly appointed Signed: Le Chi Mau translator for the Superior Court (Seal) of California, County of San Diego certify that I have translated the attached document from VIETNAMESE into ENGLISH and do certify that it is correct to the best of my ability system and judgement.
