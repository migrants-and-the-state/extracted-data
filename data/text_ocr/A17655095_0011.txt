PROVINCE OF QUEBEC DISTRICT OF MONTREAL I, the undersigned JOSEPH KLEIN, merchant, residing at 4810 Bourret Ave. Apt. 205, Montreal, being duly sworn, depose and say: 1. That my sister Dame Ilona Klein (Mrs. Armin Klein) was issue of our parents' marriage, namely, Lajos Klein and Dame Zseni Rosenbom; 2. That she was born at Nagyhalasz, Szaboles, Hungary on October 20th, 1904. 3. That the above facts are true to the best of my knowledge and belief.
