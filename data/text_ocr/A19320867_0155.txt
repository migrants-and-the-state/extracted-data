AMERICAN INSTITUTE IN TAIWAN TAIPEI OFFICE Memorandum DATE : Oct. 19, 1981 TO : Donald A. Young INS Hong Kong FROM : Douglas V. Ellice, Jr. SUBJECT: Extension for Reentry Permit It is recommended that Mrs. Wu's application be denied due to the following reasons: 1. Mrs. Wu has established a clear travel pattern over the years of brief visits to the U.S. 2. Neither she nor her husband own any property in the U. S. , and they pay no U.S. taxes. 3. Her husband is employed in Taiwan and has never even been petitioned for. He is a governmant official, and he has no firm plans to move to the U.S. Mrs. Wu's case exhibits none of the elements normally associated with "permanent residence" AIT-10-A (6/80)
