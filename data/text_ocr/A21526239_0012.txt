No. 3068 District Ahmednagar. Reference Dear Mr. Lathi, Ref: your letter dated 18th July,1957 : I state that it is known to me & some other old staff of the Belapur Company that a daughter was born on 26th July, 1933 to Mrs. G.T.Lathi, in Harigaon, Belapur Company,Limited. We tried to find out old record of birth but it is not available here, as Harigaon is now in Shrirampur Taluka & not in Kopargaon Taluka. It is very difficult to find out records which are 24 years old. I wish the grown up child, a success in her life Yours Faithfully, R .S. Jamasji. ) ESTATE SUPERINTENDENT.
