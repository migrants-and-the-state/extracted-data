THE STATE OF TEXAS COUNTY OF .EL PASO ) BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this date personally appeared Mr. Vance Segulia, who after being duly sworn upon his oth depose and says; That, he is a citizen of the United States of America, single, resides at Tornillo, Texas, that he owns and farms about 500 acres of farm land with an annual income of over $15,000.00. Affiant, further, states that, he is in need of farm help and has offered employment to Mr. Vicente de la Hoya Sanchez, who is a citizen of Mexico and a resident of Cd. Juarez, Chih., Mexico. Mr. de la Hoya will be employed as a farm hand and will be paid a weekly salary of $36.00 plus living quarters. Affiant also, states, that, if the Honorable American Consul will grant Mr. de la Hoya the necessary consular visa to enter this country legally to work, affiant will see that Mr. De la Hoya will never become a public charge to any agency of the United States of America as long as he stays under his employment. Respectfully submitted. total Mr. Vence Vence Segulin Je Segulia. SUBSCRIBED AND SWORN, to before me this 11th day of August, 1955. Caucebio B.Leon NOTARY PUBLIC, IN AND FOR THE COUNTY OF EL PASO, TEXAS.
