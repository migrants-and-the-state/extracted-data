Pierce Street Macomb, Illinois Dear Sir: This office is in receipt of your letter dated July 21, 1961, concerning the applications for nat- uralization submitted by your two sons, your wife and yourself. A Naturalization Examiner from this office will be conducting hearings on applications for natural- ization at Peoria, Illinois, on August 3, and 4, 1961. The next date thereafter, that such hearings will be conducted at Peoria, Illinois, will be sometime during the month of November or December, 1961. It is suggested, therefore, that if you do not wish to delay your application for naturalization, that you appear on August 4, 1961, at the time indicated and bring with you two witnesses other then those men- tioned in your letter.
