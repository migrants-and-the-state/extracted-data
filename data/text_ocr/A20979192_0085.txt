Box 373 Claremont, California Dear Mr. Palos: We received your letter asking us to search our records so we would be able to give you a letter stating you attended our school to help you get your citizenship papers. We have searched our records back to 1910 but cannot find your name or that of your sister. We wish to state that we are a High School and from a statement in your letter regarding your sister, we believe your record might be in the Elementary School record. Accordingly, we have sent the information regarding your names to the Nettie L. Waite Elementary School in Norwalk as they are custodian of the old records of the Norwalk Elementary School you may have at- tended. We sincerely hope they will be able to find the record and should they forward it to us, we will be only to happy to again write you. Sincerely yours, Elizabeth Wattains Elizabeth Watkins, Counselor g
