CO khong (Neu tra 1oi CO, xin giai thich o' mat sau) Ngoai ra, ti cung chua bao gio ra lenh, tro giup hoac tham gia duoi mot hinh thuc nao khac vao viec hanh ha bat cu' mot nguoi nao vi ly do chung toc, ton giao hoac chanh kien. Toi hieu tat ca nhung dieu tren day da xin va duoc cunq cap pan dich hoac duoc giai thich ve moi dieu toi chua thau hieu.
