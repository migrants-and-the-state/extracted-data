Nov. 5, 1962 482-P-27899 NA Mrs. Florance Cohen 417 N. 32nd Place, Apt. 5 Phoenix 8, Arizona Dear Madam: In reply to your letter of October 31, 1962, you are advised that in order that your petition for naturalization be transferred to a court having jurisdiction over your present place of residence, it is sug- gested that you complete the enclosed application for such transfer in quadruplicate, have it sworn to before an officer of this Service, Notary Public, or clerk of court and return the four copies to this office. Thereafter, your file will be forwarded to our office in Arizona and that office will advise you when and where to appear to take your oath of citizenship. Very truly yours, District Director By: Joseph Minton, Deputy District Director Encl. fan
