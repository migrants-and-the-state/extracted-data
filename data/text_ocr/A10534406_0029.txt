EL PASO, TEXAS 2-19-55 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN: This is to certify that the bearer Miss Mericia Maldonado is employed by me as a housemaid at a salary of $15.00 per week. Miss Maldonado has proven to be an honest and hard worker and any favors shown her will be greatly appreciated. Yours Truly Mrs Ruby Sims Mrs. Ruby Smith Sims 505 Linda Drive El Paso, Texas SUBSCRIBED AND SWORN, to before me, this the 19th. day of February 1955. James State NOTARY of PUBLIC, in and for the Texas, County of El Paso.
