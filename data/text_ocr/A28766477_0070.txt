Screened by NARA, 6/1/2023 The ZU-23 is a 23-millimeter antiaircraft gun. Each of its two barrels can fire at the rate of 800-1,000 rounds per minute. It has an effective range of more than 8,000 feet. The SA-7 is a surface-to-air missile with an infrared guidance system. The Soviets have provided the Sandinistas more than 300 SA-7 launchers, such as this one held by a Soviet soldier. The M-1939, a 37-millimeter antiaircraft gun, can fire at the rate of 160-180 rounds per minute. It has an effective range of more than 8,000 feet. There are nine M-1939 sites in the Managua area alone. Notice that in the bed of each truck, a soldier holds an SA-7 missile launcher. 27
