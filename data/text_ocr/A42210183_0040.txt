DEPARTMENT U.S. OF U.S. Department of Homeland Security U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services HOMELAND SECURITY 9650 Flair Drive, El Monte, CA 91731 November 10, 2003 MEMORANDUM TO FILE U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services FROM: Mary-Esther G. Johnson Interim Officer In Charge Citizenship El Monte SUBJECT: Mandatory IBIS checks Headquarters has conducted a batch IBIS check on all CLAIMS 4 Cases that are Scheduled for the oath ceremony on November 12, 2003. This memorandum was placed in all the files that met one of the following criteria. 1. The IBIS check did not result in a positive hit response. 2. The IBIS check resulted in a positive response, and the findings were determined to have no bearing on the applicant's eligibility for naturalization. 3. The results were determine not to be applicable to the applicant. All applicants that met the above conditions were allowed to continue to the oath ceremony.
