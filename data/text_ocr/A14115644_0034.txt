Screened by NARA, 6/29/2023 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN: This letter will introduce LOUIS CORRAL, whom I have known personally since 1920. He is a very close friend of our family. Any favors extended to Mr. Louis Corral will be appreciated by our family. We have lived in Artesia, New Mexico all our lives. I own and operate a farm here in this city. It would be a favor to me to see that this letter will help Mr. Louis Corral in any manner which he so desires. I cannot state how much his friendship means to us, The Torres family here in Artesia. MinisTerres DAVE TORRES Rt. 1 Box 118 Artesia, New Mexico 88210 dated this 27th day of April, 1973.
