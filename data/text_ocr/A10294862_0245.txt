Dear Sir: You are hereby advised that your release from custody under deportation proceedings has been authorized under the following conditions: $1000.00 Delivery Bond Enclosed you will find, in duplicate, Form I-290A, Notice of Appeal to the Board of Immigration Appeals. If you desire to file an appeal from the decision contained in this communication, these forms must be returned to this office executed, in duplicate, as soon as possible, but not later than five (5) days, (not including Sundays or holidays) after receipt of this notice. A brief or other written statement, in duplicate, in support of an appeal may be sub- mitted with the Notice of Appeal. If an appeal is taken, Notice of Appeal, properly executed, should be returned in the self-addressed envelope which is enclosed, together with fee in the amount of $10.00. Remittance should be by Postal, Express or bank Money Order, made payable to the "Treasurer of the United States, New York, New York." Very truly yours, EDW. J.
