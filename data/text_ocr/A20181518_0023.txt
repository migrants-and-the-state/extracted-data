June 29, 1964 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN: To my knowledge, my father Isador Young brought my brother, Joseph to the United States from Russia in 1905. I was born in Cincinnati, Ohio on April 27, 1909 and have resided in the United States all my life. To my knowledge, my brother Joseph was never out of the United States of America. believer Blum Mrs. Lillian Young Blum Rosans Manell Notary Public ROSARIO J.
