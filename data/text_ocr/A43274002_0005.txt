Screened by NARA, 9/27/2023 SIKRI, NARAIN D Page 3 LIN9818052148 Please be advised that a lawful permanent resident of the United States in possession of Form I-151 or Form I-551 who intends to reenter the United States within one year of his/her departure, does not require a reentry permit to reenter. However, if a lawful permanent resident of the United States seeks to reenter the United States after an absence of over one year, and does not possess a reentry permit, he/she should contact a United States Consulate for further information regarding his/her possible options.
