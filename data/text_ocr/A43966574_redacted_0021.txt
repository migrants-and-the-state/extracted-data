RUPEES (0) AFFIDAVIT I, Muzaffar Khan Raja son of Ali Akbar Khan, resident of village Perchhan Post Office Kotli Sattiyan, Tehsil Murree, District Rawalpindi, do hereby solemnly affirm and declare as under:- a) That I belong to a remote village. No Town/Municipal Committee existed in my village at the time of my birth. b) I still remember my year of birth. I was born at the above village in 1903 which fact I knew just after passing a few years from my parents. c) The same year of birth has therefore been entered in my National Identity Card so as to make it proof of my birth for all purposes whereever I am required to submit this information. d) Since my birth I have shown the same year of birth and no change has been made. This is my real year of birth and I stand responsible for it at present and in future whereever I am asked about the year of my birth. Exact date is however not known to me. due to passage of a long time of as many as 90 years, The above_mentioned statement given by me is true to the best of my knowledge and belief and nothing has been concealed therefrom.
