Screened by NARA, 7/28/2023 ATTACHMENT #8 ALL APPLICANTS If you were arrested at any time and any place (in or outside the U. S.), you must bring to your interview certified copies of the following documents for each arrest and a Police Background Clearance: 1) Arresting officer's report(s) or record(s) of indictment; 2) Court's disposition of case(s), i.e., conviction, nolle pros, adjudication withheld, no information, case closed, etc.; 3) Level of arrest, i.e., filony or misdemeanor; 4) Record showing sentence(s) imposed and time(s) served or probation information; and 5) Letter from Probation Official showing probationary period satisfactorily completed. NOTE: SEALED ARREST(S) AND/OR CONVICTION(S) MUST BE REOPENED! If you were arrested outside the Miami (Dade County) area, you must bring to your interview a Police Backgraound Clearance from each area, as well. If you reside in the Miami-Dade area, you must bring a Miami-Dade (formerly Metro-Dade) Police Background Clearance.
