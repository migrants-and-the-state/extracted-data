Q. What is his name ? A. Alberto Acosta. Q. When you immigrated to the United States, July 27, 1955, did you actually come to the United States tolive? A. Yes. Q. Where did you take up residence in the United States? A. On Alameda Street. Q. When you immigrated to the United States in 1955, what did you do with your home in Juarez? A. It was rented. Q. How long did you stay in the United States after you immigrated July 27, 1955? A. We lived here about four years after I immigrated. Q. If you immigrated in 1955 and lived in El Paso four years, that would have been about 1959, wouldn't it? A. About then. &. Where did you go to live in 1959? A. We came to Juarez to live. Q. Why did you and your husband move to Juarez to live in 1959? A. Because we had our home there. Q. Have you and your husband had your home continuously in Juarez, Mexico since 1959? A. Yes. Q. Have you resided continuously in Juarez, Mexico since 1959? A. Yes. Q. Yesterday when I interviewed you, did you tell me you had only worked in El Paso a time or two since you returned to Juarez to live? A. Yes I told you that. I only iron for my sister once a week. I don't work. Q. What is the name of your sister? A. Dolores Labrado de Acosta. Q. Where does she live? A. 4431 Bliss. Q. I note you handed me a piece of paper with this address written on it.
