Easey Dany INVESTIGATOR SYNOPSIS SUBJECT WONG Gim Lee testified on August 1, 1966 that he is a native and citizen of China; never had any claim to United States citizenship and never has been inspected or admitted to the United States as an alien. A copy of his statement is attached to his file. SUBJECT LEE Suey Gin made a sworn statement on August 4, 1966 that she is a native and citizen of China and that she entered the United States on one occasion as a spouse of a United States citizen. She testified she knew that her husband was using a false surname in this country, but did not know that he was not a citizen of the United States. A copy of her affidavit is attached to the file.
