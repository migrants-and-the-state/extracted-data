Screened by NARA, 2/10/2023 khan truong cua Quoc Gia. Ban CO thuoc loai nao trong cac loai tren day khong? CO khong (Neu tra 1oi CO, xin giai thich o' mat sau) Ngoai ra, toi cung chua bao gio ra lenh, tro giup hoac tham gia duoi mot hinh thu'c nao khac vao viec hanh ha bat cui mot nguoi nao vi ly do chung toc, ton giao hoac chanh kien. Toi hieu tat ca nhung dieu tren day, da xin va duoc cunq cap ban dich hoac duoc giai thich ve moi dieu toi chua thau hieu. Chu ky day du va dich thuc cua Ung Vien c Ung Vien CO ten tren day da trinh dien va tuyen the truo'c mat toi tai Ngay (Ngay, thang, nam) Ten, ho Thong dich vien (viet bang chu in hoa) Chu ky cua Thong Dich Vien Can Chu ky va Chuc Vu cua Vien Chuc G-646
