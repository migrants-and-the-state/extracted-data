Screened by NARA, 8/11/2023 FILING A CLAIM For help in filing a claim your beneficiary should contact a Human Resources representative. If a claim is denied, your beneficiary will receive a written statement of the reasons for the denial. Included in the statement will be the name and address of the person the beneficiary should contact to have the denial reviewed. TERMINATION OF COVERAGE Your Supplemental Life Insurance coverage will end on the earliest of the following: When your Honeywell employment ends due to resignation, layoff or discharge The last day of the last period for which you have made the required contribution and you are not eligible for retiree coverage On the day you retire - if you were insured in the Plan for less than one year However, if you are certified totally and permanently dis- abled for the Honeywell Pension/Retirement plan in which you are a participant, coverage will continue without your paying any further premiums. Coverage will be continued as long as you are totally and permanently disabled, but in no case, past age 65. BENEFITS AFTER TERMINATION OF COVERAGE In the event of your death within 31 days after your group coverage ends, the full amount of your Supplemental Life Insurance benefit will still be paid to your beneficiary. 6 PUBLICATION NUMBER 59-7123, JUNE, 1985
