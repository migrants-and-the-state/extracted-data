Central Avenue Los ANGELES 21, CALIFORNIA MADISON 5-0304 - MADISON 3-4003 January 23, 1975 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN: This letter will certify that I, LOUIS MARTIN delivered groceries and other food supplies to the Maharaja Snack Bar in December, 1971. These deliveries were made to enable the Maharaja Snack Bar to open for business. The total amount of food delivered at that time was worth Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00). This company subsequently delivered food to the Maharaja Snack Bar totaling Four Thousand, Eight Hundred Dollars ($4,800.00) during the year of 1972. frien Martin LOUIS MARTIN Owner
