EL PASO NATIONAL BANK SAM D. YOUNG CAPITAL FUNDS OVER $7,000,000 HOMER A.JACOBS PRESIDENT EXECUTIVE VICE-PRESIDENT ELPASO, TEXAS June 13, 1955 Honorable Robert Y. Brown American Consul American Consulate Post Office Box #3 El Paso, Texas Dear Mr. Brown: Mr. Jack R. McNutt has been known to the members of our staff for more than ten years. He has utilized the facilities of the El Paso National Bank in connection with his needs for banking service. He has carried both checking and savings accounts here. We have had occasion to fill his loan demands. Through all of these associations we know Mr. McNutt to be of excellent business and personal reputation. Mr. McNutt is an owner of McNutt Oil and Refining Company and as such is prominent in business in El Paso. In our opinion, he will promptly meet his contractual obligations. Mr. McNutt tells us that he is sponsoring the application for visa of an alien. This letter is submitted in connection with this sponsorship. Sincerely, JACK V. CURLIN Vice President JVC/sns
