UNIVERSITY OF MINNESOTA GRADUATE SCHOOL MINNEAPOLIS 14 OFFICE OF THE DEAN July 19, 1951 Mr. Rex Griffin Immigration and Naturalization Service 1014 New Post Office Building St. Paul 1, Minnesota Dear Mr. Griffin: Mr. Maoling Liu received his Master of Science in Electri- cal Engineering degree from the University of Minnesota in December, 1950.
