SUBJECT RAMON WONG was interviewed January 8, 1969 and a sworn statement taken, copy attached as Exibit "C" In that statement Ex "C" he admitted that he has never met Mr. LOO, the sponser of his sixth preference visa and the signer of the form ES 575 under which he secured exemption form the excluding provisions of Section 212 Aa) (14). When questioned as to why he did not report to his employment on entry into the United States he stated that he needed to rest and to recuperate from his many years of work in Mexico, before accepting employment in the United States. He stated that it is still his intention toaccept employment with his sponser in El Centro but personal business will keep him occupied for about six months.
