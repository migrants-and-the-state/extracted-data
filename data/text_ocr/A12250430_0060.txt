Screened by NARA, 9/18/2023 August 27, 1948. In reply refer to W X130-Wong Guck On Mr. Watson. B. Miller, Commissioner, Immigration and Naturalization Service, Washington 25, D.C. Sir: The Department is endeavoring to verify the American citizenship of Wong Guck On , who alleges that he was born at Sun Loong Vill. , Toy San, Kwangtung, on China July 17, 1918 , and who appears to have obtained Immi oration Form 430 vor Certificate of Identity No. 67098 issued at San Pedro, Cal. on approximately Oct. 21, 1932. It will be appreciated if you will verify the issuance of the document mentioned above and will state whether the person concerned was admitted to the United States as an American citizen, and, if so, upon what date. It is requested that a report be obtained from your field office by air mail. Very truly yours, R.B. Shipley, Chief, Passport Division Enclosure: Copy of this letter. DSL-323 4-21-48 PD-179
