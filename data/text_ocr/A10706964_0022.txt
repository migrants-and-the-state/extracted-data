John T. Bean Farm 7421 Dale Road El Paso, Texas October 1, 1956 To Whom It May Concern: This is to certify that Enrique Mota is employed as farm worker on my farm. He receives $2.05 per 100 pounds for picking 1517-C cotton and he receives $3.00 per loo pounds for picking S-1 Pima cotton. The farm furnishes him quarters, garden, and utilities. Yours very truly, John John T. T. Bean Bean, Owner
