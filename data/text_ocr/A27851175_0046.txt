Screened by NARA, 9/22/2023 REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM EXTRACT FROM THE RECORDS DEPOSITED COURT OF PEACE : SAIGON can AT THE CLERK'S OFFICE OF SAIGON COURT OF PEACE No. 5731 Date : Oct.05,1961 DOCUMENT IN LIEU OF BIRTH CERTIFICATE FOR : LU CAM TIEU In the year of 1961 , the 5th day of October , at 9:00 In front of us, NGUYEN VAN DOANH , President of Saigon Court of Peace, at our office, 115 Nguyen Hue Blvd., and PHAN VAN TU , Court Clerk, as assistant, HAVE APPEARED 1. HUYNH AN 2.- PHAM CANG 3.- HA THI These witnesses, after being sworn, affirmed they perfectly know that : LU CAM TIEU, male, was born in 1915 at Kwangtung (China) to Mr. LU TRAC (deceased) and Mrs. AN PHAT (living). TRANSLATION And the reason for which he could not apply for an ex- tract of birth certificate is tibatix due to the geographical scission of the country. The witnesses have acknowledged all the above, therefore we issued this substitute of birth certificate to the applicant for use within the law, in accordance with the Nov. 17, 1947 Decree of the Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of South Vietnam. The articles 241 and 245 of the Revised Criminal Code punishing false testimony on civil matters from 2 to 5 years imprisonment and fine from 1000 to 3000 piastres have been read to the witnesses and the applicant. We, the Court Clerk and the witnesses have signed below after all the above have been re-read.
