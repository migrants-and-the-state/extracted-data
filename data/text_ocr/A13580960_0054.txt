SOCIAL WORKER February 1, 1963 LOS ANGELES 33, CALIFORNIA ANGELUS 1-2047 Mrs. Elizabeth Hyppolite 4816 W. 21 St. Los Angeles 16, California Dear Mrs. Hyppolite: Attached hereto is a letter containing the list of documents. I am also enclosing the request for your mother to appear before the Immigration authorities and a copy of the letter we sent the American Consul forwarding the preliminary application forms and requesting an appointment for final review of the application. Please show the Immigration authorities both of these papers when your mother appears for her interview. Your mother may appear before the Immigration Service any time Monday through Friday, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. You may tell Mrs. Solder that I expect your mother's appointment within sixty days. Very truly yours, Delfino Varela, Social Worker enc.
