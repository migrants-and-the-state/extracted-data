ROMAN CATHOLIC DIOCESE OF BROOKLYN POST OFFICE BOX C BROOKLYN, NEW YORK 11202-3604 February 28, 1987 THE CHANCERY Immigration and Naturalization Service 26 Federal Plaza New York, New York 10278 Dear Sir: This is to certify that, according to information supplied to us by his legitimate ecclesiastical superior, the Reverend Edgard Delouis is a bona fide Roman Catholic Priest. He was ordained for the Archdiocese of Port-au- Prince, Haiti, on June 11, 1933. For reasons of health, he has received the permission of his legitimate superior to reside in the United States. In view of the fact that a certificate of ordina- tion is not available for Father Delouis, may I respect- fully ask that this letter be accepted in its stead. With gratitude for your kind consideration in this matter, and with every best wish, I am Very truly yours, William Hoppe Reverend William M. Hoppe Vice Chancellor WMH/jb
