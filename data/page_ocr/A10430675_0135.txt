I, Bliss Mitchell Wiant, do hereby certify that on the twenty-second of January, 1937 at Yenching University, Peking, China, Thelma J. Bosland, a citizen of the United States of America, aged twenty-nine, born in Waubay, South Dakota and now residing in Yenching University, Peking, China and Maoling Liu, a citizen of China, aged thirty-three years, born in Shantung Province, China and now residing in Yenching University, Peking, China were united in marriage by me. In testimony whereof, I have hereunto set my hand at Yenching University, Peking, China, this twenty-second day of January, A.D. 1937. Blin Mitchel Weaut Bliss Mitchell Wiant Elder of The Methodist Episcopal Church
