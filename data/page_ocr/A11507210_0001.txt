PLEASE QUOTE NO. m32731/883 The Consul General for the United States of America, 7 Wynyard Street, SYDNEY. Dear Sir, Louis Sanitrik of 167 Macquarie Street, Sydney, who is understood to be desirous of entering the United States of America, attended at the Fingerprint Section of this Department on the 29th October, 1958, and consented to his fingerprints being taken. These fingerprints were classified and a search of the records of the Fingerprint Section revealed that they were identical with those of Lewis Sanitrik who appeared before Central Court of Petty Sessions on the 8th November, 1954, on a charge of offensive manner and was fined £5, in default 10 days hard labour. He also appeared at the same Court on the 27th May, 1958, on a charge of offensive behaviour and was fined £4, in default 8 days hard labour. Yours faithfully, gle Smith G.L.
