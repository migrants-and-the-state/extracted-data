American Consulate, Peiping, China, October 11, 1946 I, Fulton Freeman, Consul of the United States of America within and for the consular district of Peiping, China, hereby certify that the documents required by the provisions of the Immigration Act of 1924, as amended, to be attached to the nonquota immigration visa of Mr. Maoling LIU are not available for the following reasons: 1. Birth certificates were not being issued by the Chinese authorities in the Province of Shantung, China, at the date of Mr. Liu's birth. 2. As Mr. Liu has never had any military service, and was never arrested, there was no occasion for recording such matters in his case, and no such records are available.
