Reference is made to the application for adjustment of status filed by you on March 16, 1987. You seek adjustmenet of status as a special immigrant under Section 101(a)(27)(c)(1) of the Immigration and Nationality Act. According to this section of the act, the term "special immigrant" means- an immigrant who continuously for at least two years immediately preceding the time of his application for admission to the United States has been and who seeks to enter the United States solely for the purpose of carrying on the vocation of minister of a religious denomination On your biographical data sheet, you state that you have been unemployed from September, 1983 to the present. Since you have not carried on the vocation of priest for the two years preceding your application for admission you failed to qualify as a special immigrant. Your application for adjustment of status is therefore, denied.
