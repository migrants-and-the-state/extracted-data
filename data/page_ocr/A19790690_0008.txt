Aherm District Director Immigration & Naturalization Service 815 Airport Way South Seattle, WA 98134 Dear Mr. Aherm: We are writing this letter to support our mother's applica- tion for permanent residence in the United States. She has stayed with us and we have totally supported her financially and will continue to support her while she chooses this country as her residence. We guarantee that she will never become a public charge. We hope that you will give her a favorable consideration. Thank you very much.
