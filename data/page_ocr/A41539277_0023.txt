SINGH Ref No Pastor August 31,1988 Date TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN This is to certify that Mrs Katherine Theodore is well known to me and she is an active member of the Christ Methodist Church, Delhi-54. I further would like to certify that Henrietta Samudre is her real daughter. Henrietta Samudre borne, brought up, educated and got married here. But now she is in U.S.A. with her family. She has come to India to visit her old mother. Very recently her brother also died and there is no one to look after the old aged mother. She wants to take her mother with her to the U.S. A. I wish all success for them. Helligh Rev. S.S. Singh Pastor Christ Methodist Charts 1. Butler Road, Delhi-54.
