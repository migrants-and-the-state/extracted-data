Screened by NARA, 9/14/2023 EXTRACT OF THE BIRTH CERTIFICATE OF THE COMMUNE OF PORT-DE-PAIX,M DEPOSED IN THE NATIONAL ARCHIVES OF THE REPUBLIC LIBERTY EQUALITY FRATERNITY REPUBLIC OF HAITI No 10528 SERIAL WWW THE YEAR NINETEEN HUNDRED SIX, THE 103TH OF THE INDEPENDENCE AND ON MONDAY APRIL TWENTIETH AT TEN O'CLOCK IN THE MORNING. BEFORE US, JACQUES PIERRE, CIVIL REGISTRAR OF THE COMMUNE OF PORT-DE-PAIX. UNDERSIGNED: HAD APPEARED MR CETOUTE LOUIS XVI, LABORER, RESIDING AND DOMICILED IN MOUSTIQUE, 3RD RURAL SECTION OF THE COMMUNE OF PORT-DE-PAIX. WHO HAS PRESENTED TO US A MALE CHILD. WHOM HE DECLARED TO BE HIS LEGITIMATE SON BORN IN HIS DOMICILE ON MARCH EIGHTEENTH NINETEEN HUNDRED SIX AT ELEVEN O'CLOCK IN THE EVENING. FROM THE LADY ORELIA LAURENSAINT, LABORER, RESIDING AND DOMICILED IN MOUSTIQUE. TO WHOM THE CHILD WAS GIVEN THE FIRST NAME OF "MICHEL A.K.A TRAGE". THIS ACT WAS DONE IN OUR OFFICE IN PRESENCE OF LUCIEN LOUIS XVI AND LOUISE LAURENSAINT, BOTH ADULTS RESIDING AND DOMICILED AT THE SAME PLACE. WITNESSES CHOSEN AND BROUGHT BY THE APPEARER. LECTURE WAS DONE BY US, WE HAVE SIGNED IT ALONE WITH THE FATHER AND WITHOUT THE WITNESSES FOR THE REASON OF NOT KNOWING TO DO SO. SIGNED: JACQUES PIERRE PORT-AU-PRINCE, THIS JULY 12TH, 1987 MENTION IN THE MARGIN : BIRTH CERTIFICATE OF "MICHEL A.K.A. TRAGE LOUIS XVI" BORN ON MARCH 18TH, 1906.
