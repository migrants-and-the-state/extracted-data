Screened by NARA, 6/13/2023 AFFIDAVIT FOR MARRIAGE This affidavit is pertaining to the marriage of Mr. Ali Warsame and Mrs. Ardo botan Guray. I, Abucar Mohamud Liban, born 5/20/1942 in Garowe, Somalia and presently residing 9101 Meadow Rue Line, Annandale, VA 22003 certify the following: I. My relationship with Mr. Warsame is a family friend and former business partner of Mr. Warsame's father. II. I have a personnel knowledge of Mr. Ali Warsame and Mrs. Ardo Botan Guray's marriage. I lived the same city with them in central Somalia (Galkio) from 1955 to 1967 and later in the capital city (Mogadisho). I was not present at their wedding but was invited to a dinner to commemorate their wedding. I can only recall the year of their marriage which was 1957. III. Mr. Warsame's family were always respected permanent family in the community and also Mr. Warsame's father served as community leader (elder) for many years in Galkio as well as Mogadisho. I am very happy to provide this information for Mr. Warsame and will be willing to provide more if needed. County or Fortax Abucar Mohamud Liban Commonwealth of Virginic subscribed and sworn to before as This 9th day of OCTOBIOR 1992 ABUCAR MOHAMMD UPAN Amale by Stre My Notary Public Commission Expires 11/30 1995
