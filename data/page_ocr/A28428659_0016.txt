Immigration and Naturalization Service 26 Federal Plaza New York, New York 10278 To Whom It May Concern: I wish to inform you that Reverend Edgard F. DeLouis, a priest of the Diocese of Port-au-Prince Haiti, will be assigned to the Roman Catholic Church of Saints Joachem and Anne in the Diocese of Brooklyn. During this assignment Father DeLouis will reside at the rectory which is situated near the Church. It is located at 218-26 105th Avenue, Queens Village, New York 11429. During the period of his ministry in this or any other Parish of this Diocese, he will receive the usual stipend of a Diocesan priest, together with room and board, and he will not be permitted to become a public charge to the United States Government or any of its subdivisions.
