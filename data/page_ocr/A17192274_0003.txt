To apply for the certification, attached Forms ES-575A and B must be completed in accordance with the enclosed instructions. The com- pleted Forms ES-575A and B, and the evidence of the alien's qualifications, must be filed in the local state employment service office. The Forms ES-575A and B and the evidence will be returned to you, with the certification stamped on Form ES-575 if a certification is issued. You may then file the petition at this office, attaching all the papers and documents returned to you by the Department of Labor. You have indicated that you do not intend to seek employment. You must furnish evidence that you have sufficient funds or other means of maintaining yourself in this country.
