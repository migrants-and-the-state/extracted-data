EL PASO, TEXAS 2-26-55 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN: This is to certify that the bearer Mr. Amadeo Eulalio Elizalde is employed by me as S plasterer at a salary of $60.00 per week. Yours Truly Ramon More Ramon Montes 907 Hammett El Paso, Texas SUBSCRIBED AND SWORN, to before me, this the 26th. day of Feb. 1955. NOTARY PUBLIC, in and for the State of Texas, County of El Paso
