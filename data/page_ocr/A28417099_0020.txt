Immigration Judge Upon the basis of respondent's admissions I have determined that he is deportable on the charge(s) in the Order to Show Cause. Respondent has made application solely for voluntary departure in lieu of deportation. ORDER: It is ordered that in lieu of an order of deportation respondent be granted voluntary departure without expense to the Government on or before NOVEMBER 30, 1990 (Date) or any extension beyond such date as may be granted by the district director for the Immigration and Naturalization Service, and under such conditions as the district director shall direct. IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that if respondent fails to depart when and as required, the privilege of voluntary departure shall be withdrawn without further notice or proceedings and the following order shall thereupon become immediately effective: respondent shall be deported from the United States to NICARAGUA on the charge(s) contained in the Order to Show Cause.
