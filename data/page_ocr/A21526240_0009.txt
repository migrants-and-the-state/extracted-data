SINDIA GINGE TOUR FOR THREE RUPEES THREE RUPEES signed HERINE 9 In majley his (hrs) 361900 of -grs 10P. 1RUPEE SPAISE Affidavit 1) In the court of Taluka Magistrate at Bhusawal Maharashtra State India. I Ladhe Narayandas Gopaldas age 76, occupation Agriculture, residing at Varadsim India, do here by an solemn affirmation state as follow. That my younger Sister Ladhe Ayodhabai Gopaldas born at Varadsim India on 18th July 1907 was married in the year 1919 at Varadsim India to Lathi Govindram Totaram born at Amoda India on 22nd December 1903. The exact date of marriage in the year 1919, could not be - traced, and hence not mentioned. Since her marriage her name is Mrs. Lathi Ayodhabai Govindram. We all brothers and sister used to live with our parents in a joint family in the year 1919. So I had to attend to many duties during my younger sister's marriage. What has been stated above is quite true and correct to the best of my knowledge, and belief and information, in token of which I set my hand below this on 12th June 1975 by signing this affidavit at Bhusawal India.
