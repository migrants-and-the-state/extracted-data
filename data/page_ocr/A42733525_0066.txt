Screened by NARA, 9/26/2023 NOTICE Effective immediately, the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service no longer routinely requires submission of original documents or "certified" copies of original documents. Uncertified photocopies of such documents (including naturalization certificates and Alien Registration Cards) are accepted as long as the originals are available.
